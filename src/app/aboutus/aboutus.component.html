What is Goal-cator?

Goal-cator is a fully customized game designed as a problem solution as per requirement of our final year Software Engineering 2 assignment meant to solve a problem currently being faced by NUST. Part of the solution is to create awareness and the other part is to come up with a game that will help stakeholders to learn more about the NUST strategic plan and to understand the strategic direction of the plan and this.....this is GOAL-CATOR. Derived from the two important words in this project, goal and indicator.

Designed and developed by: Toini Jonas

Student Number: 217089135

Why Goal-cator?

Namibia university of Science and Technology (NUST) want to create awareness about the strategic direction of the university. The university has a guiding document called strategic plan in which all goals and the key performance indicators (KPI) are outlined. The university community and the public do not effectively read and get to know the plan. In an effort to encourage the public to engage the plan more, the department of institutional planning approached me with an idea of gamifying the plan. Goal-cator fits and suit all the requirements specified which include and not limited to; will assist the stakeholders to learn more about the plan and get to memorize and understand the strategic direction of the plan.

Is Goal-cator free?

Goal-cator is free to all NUST stakeholders as the main reason of the game is to promote awareness of the NUST strategic plan, which is available for free and you can find it here.
